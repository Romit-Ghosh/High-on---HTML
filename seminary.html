SEMINARY HILLS



About Seminary Hill

Seminary Hill is a small hill located about 6 km from Nagpur city. It is a favorite of visitors as it offers a panoramic view of the entire Nagpur city. Named after St. Charles Seminary, it is well established with walking paths, making it a perfect place to enjoy a peaceful trek and spend time in peace. Pleasant weather, panoramic views and serenity leave one refreshed.

Interesting facts about Seminary Hill

1. This place is a nature lover and photographer's paradise who is ready to shower his/her generosity.
2. The hill is named after St. Charles Madrasa.
3. The Japanese Garden at the foothills and the Satpura Botanical Garden on the hill attract a large number of tourists.
4. An astonishing view of Nagpur can be seen from the top of Seminary Hill.
5. Seminary Hill is a sea of green and controlled forest offers wonderful walks for morning walks and joggers.
6. Government bodies like Air Force Office, CGO Complex and TV Tower are also located in this beautiful place.
